Brainstormer - Unbolt knowledge with the speed of thought

Brainstormer is the annual quiz competition organised by Phoenix - the official tech-club of Netaji Subhash Engineering College. Brainstormer has seen a surge in the number of enthusiastic participants each year, with students from notable institutions taking part from all over the country. In 2016, one of the country's leading and most sought after quizmaster, Mr. Barry O' Brien hosted this significant event, with a participation of more than 500 students. This year it is going to be even Bigger and Better!
